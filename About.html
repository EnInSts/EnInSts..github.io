Game

Operation Labyrinthus will be a first-person shooter. Players will try to reach the
top of a tower of mazes, by killing monsters, different bosses and each other. This idea
came to us while doing one of our weekly TP’s during which we had to create a program
that could solve mazes, hence the tower of mazes

Group

Our group aims to provide a different experience from other first-person shooters
where the player’s only target is to kill enemies with no actual cognitive task being
performed by the player. For the purpose of completely mesmerizing the player with
our gameplay we placed him in a maze so he must think not only of where his enemies
might be coming from next but also about how he will solve the maze so that he can
move on to the next level.
