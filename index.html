OUR PROJECT

As part of the second semester project we have decided to create a two-player maze runner, beating each other to reach the top of the tower. On their journey there, they will encounter mobs and bosses trying to slow them down.
May the fastest man WIN

OUR INSPIRATION

Creating a first person shooter is nothing new. Ever since our younger days we have been playing these types of games. Being Computer Science students makes it all the more true. Games to the likes of Call Of Duty, Apex Legends, Overwatch or even Doom where and are still part of our core being. We have then decided to combine those years of good memory into an FPS game but, with our own twist.

OUR PURPOSE

The aim of this project is to create a fun and intuitive game for everyone to play. We want anyone to be able to join the game and play at his hearts content, while both testing his gaming ability and his memory. Engineers in Suits has not been created to gain and fame and riches but to test our imagination and creativity in a project that uses the few months we have had at Epita.
